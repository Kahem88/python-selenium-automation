how to make a PB&J sandwich

Ingredients:

  • two slices of whole bread
  • peanut butter
  • grape jelly
  • a spoon or a butter knife to spread the ingredients

instructions:

  1. add a scoop of jelly with one of the utensils and spread the jelly to the slice of bread
  2. add a scoop of peanut butter using one of the utensils and spread the peanut butter to the other slice of bread
  3. after applying the peanut butter and the jelly to the bread combine both breads together to create the sandwich
